How you opt in

You opt in through any of these paths:

  1. You text us first at (360) 284-1554 — your first message is your opt-in.
  2. You complete our website contact or trade form and check the box consenting to texts.
  3. You provide your mobile number in person and sign or verbally agree, with the consent logged in our CRM.

Providing your mobile number solely to complete a vehicle purchase, financing application, or service transaction is NOT by itself consent to receive marketing texts. We do not add you to texting from a purchased or third-party list. Consent is non-transferable and will not be assigned to any other company.

Opt out — STOP

Reply STOP (also OPT OUT, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, QUIT, or REVOKE) at any time to cancel. You will receive one confirmation message — with no marketing or promotional content — and then no further texts. You may also opt out by any other reasonable means — call or email us at (360) 825-2200 / andrewg@gamblinmotors.com — and we honor all opt-out requests within 10 business days. We record every opt-out in our CRM and apply it across all text programs we operate on your behalf. Replying STOP (or any keyword above, or any other reasonable means) cancels both text messages and any automated or AI-assisted calls from this program. We may still reach you by live human phone call or email solely to follow up on an open inquiry you started; we will not send automated texts or place automated or AI-assisted calls to you after you reply STOP.
