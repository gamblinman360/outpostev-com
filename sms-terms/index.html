SMS Program Terms

Effective June 9, 2026

1. Program description

"Text Outpost EV" is a conversational customer-care texting program operated by Gamblin LLC, doing business as Art Gamblin Motors and Outpost EV ("we," "us"), located at 205 Roosevelt Ave, Enumclaw, WA 98022. Messages are sent from and received at (360) 284-1554. The program answers customer questions, takes test-drive requests, and records messages for our team. After-hours conversations are handled by Gambo, our AI assistant, with follow-up from our human team during business hours.

2. Opting in

You can consent to receive text messages from us in one of two ways:

  • By sending a text message to (360) 284-1554 — initiating a conversation is your consent to receive our replies; or
  • By submitting your mobile number and checking the SMS consent checkbox on our contact form at gamblinmotors.com/contactus.aspx.

Consent to receive text messages is not a condition of purchasing any goods or services. We do not purchase contact lists and we do not send unsolicited messages.

3. Message frequency

Message frequency varies based on your conversation. This is a conversational program — we reply to what you ask and follow up on requests you make. We do not send recurring marketing blasts.

4. Fees

Message and data rates may apply, depending on your mobile carrier and plan. Contact your carrier for details. Carriers are not liable for delayed or undelivered messages.

5. Opting out

Reply STOP to any message to cancel at any time. STOPALL, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, and QUIT also work. After you opt out, you will receive one final message confirming your opt-out, and nothing further. Reply START to re-subscribe.

6. Help and support

Reply HELP to any message for assistance, call us at (360) 825-2200, or visit us at 205 Roosevelt Ave, Enumclaw, WA 98022.

7. Privacy

Our Privacy Policy describes how we handle your information. In short: no mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.
