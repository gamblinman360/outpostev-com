Privacy Policy — Art Gamblin Motors / Outpost EV

Last updated: June 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Gamblin LLC ("Art Gamblin Motors," "Outpost EV," "we," "us"), located at 1047 Roosevelt Ave E, Enumclaw, WA 98022, collects, uses, shares, and protects your information across our website, phone, and text channels. Questions: andrewg@gamblinmotors.com or (360) 825-2200.

1. Who we are

We are a Washington used-electric-vehicle dealership. We sell and service vehicles and, for customers who request it, we arrange financing — which makes us a "financial institution" under federal law for the limited purpose of how we handle your financial information (see §7).

2. Information we collect

  • You give us: name, phone number, email, ZIP, vehicle of interest, trade/service details, and the content of the calls and texts you exchange with us.
  • For financing (only if you apply): Social Security number, date of birth, income, employment, driver's license, and bank/credit details — collectively "nonpublic personal information" (NPI) under §7.
  • How your financing information is handled — not by our AI or CRM. When you apply for financing, your credit application and the sensitive details in it (including your Social Security number) are entered into and processed through secure third-party finance platforms — RouteOne captures the online credit application on our website, and Dealertrack, CUDL, and our F&I compliance/credit tools (Fairway) are used to run credit and submit your application to lenders. Our automated assistant ("Gambo") and our customer-management (CRM) system do not receive, process, or store your Social Security number or financing data — that information goes directly into the secure finance portals (see §7).
  • Automatically online: IP address, browser/device data, and cookie/analytics data (§2a).
  • Phone: call metadata and, where disclosed, call recordings and transcripts (§6).
  • From third parties: credit bureaus and consumer-reporting agencies (financing only).

2a. Cookies and tracking technologies

We currently use self-hosted, first-party analytics to understand site usage. We do not currently run Google Analytics or Google/Meta (Facebook/Instagram) remarketing pixels. If we introduce third-party analytics or retargeting pixels in the future, this section will be updated before they go live, and you will be able to opt out through your browser's cookie settings or, for Google specifically, https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout. We do not use tracking data to identify your mobile number without your consent.

3. How we use it

To respond to your calls, texts, and inquiries (including via our automated assistant); schedule and confirm appointments; process and arrange financing you request; maintain records of our communications; improve our service; and comply with law. We do not sell your personal information.

4. AI-powered communications

Your calls, texts, and online chats may be handled by our automated AI assistant ("Gambo"), including after hours or when our team is busy. On every AI-assisted interaction — whether by phone call, SMS/text, or online chat — Gambo identifies itself as an automated AI assistant for Art Gamblin Motors (Gamblin LLC) at the very beginning of that interaction, regardless of time of day or staffing level, and you may reach a human team member at any time. The assistant captures your request and routes it to our team. It does not make binding commitments on pricing, financing terms, or vehicle availability — a human confirms those. Gambo does not collect or process Social Security numbers, credit-application data, or other sensitive financial information; financing is handled only through our secure finance portals (see §2).

5. SMS / text messaging

How you consent (opt in). When you text us first, or when you provide your mobile number AND separately and explicitly agree to receive texts from us, you consent to receive texts about your inquiry. Providing a mobile number solely to complete a vehicle purchase, financing application, or service transaction does NOT by itself constitute consent to receive marketing or promotional text messages.

Mobile-information sharing. No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Sharing with subcontractors that provide support services (such as our messaging/telephony platform) is permitted solely to operate our messaging service. Text-messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties.

First-party and non-transferable. Consent to receive texts from us is obtained directly by us, is not a condition of any purchase, and will not be used by any other company to message you.

Transactional vs. marketing. Replies and updates about your specific inquiry (e.g., appointment confirmations) are sent under your express consent. Promotional or marketing texts and calls are sent only to customers who have given prior express written consent, captured separately at the point of sale or via a separate request — we record the exact disclosure shown to you, a timestamp, and your explicit agreement, and store it in our CRM.

Automated technology. We do not currently send outbound marketing texts or place automated or AI-voice marketing calls. If and when we launch such a program, messages or calls may be placed using an automatic telephone dialing system (ATDS), prerecorded voice, or artificial-intelligence voice technology, and we will first obtain and separately store your prior express written consent.

Frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply.

Opt out anytime by replying STOP (also OPT OUT, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, QUIT, REVOKE) — you will get one non-promotional confirmation message and no further texts. Reply HELP for help. You may also opt out by any other reasonable means (call, email, or the contact below); we honor opt-out requests within 10 business days. We record every opt-out in our CRM and apply it to all text programs we operate on your behalf. Replying STOP also stops any automated or AI-assisted calls from us; we may still reach you by live human phone call or email solely to follow up on an open inquiry you started. See our SMS Terms.

6. Call recording (Washington)

Washington is an all-party-consent state (RCW 9.73.030). Calls to or from us may be recorded for quality, training, and documentation. Before the conversation continues, an automated announcement informs you that the call is being recorded; by continuing the call after that announcement, you consent to the recording under RCW 9.73.030. Recordings are retained for 90 days, then deleted or rendered irrecoverable, and are accessible only to authorized personnel.

7. Financial Privacy Notice — Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act (GLBA)

Because we arrange financing, we are a "financial institution" under the federal GLBA (regulated for this purpose by the FTC) and must tell you how we collect, share, and protect your nonpublic personal information (NPI). A complete GLBA Financial Privacy Notice is provided to every financing customer on paper at the time of their transaction and is available on request.

  • Information we collect: name, address, SSN, date of birth, income, employment, credit history, driver's license, and bank/credit details — from your credit application and from consumer-reporting agencies.
  • Information we share, and with whom: with the lenders and finance companies to whom we submit your application on your behalf, and with service providers (such as our dealership-management and software vendors) that help us operate. We do not sell your personal financial information to unaffiliated marketing companies.
  • Your NPI and opt-out: We share your NPI only as necessary to process and complete the financing you request (with lenders/finance companies) and with service providers who help us operate — sharing that falls within federal exceptions for which no opt-out applies (16 CFR §§ 313.13, 313.14). We do not share your NPI with nonaffiliated companies for their own marketing, so there is no separate GLBA opt-out for you to exercise under our current practices. Questions: andrewg@gamblinmotors.com / (360) 825-2200.
  • How we protect it: we maintain a written information-security program consistent with the FTC Safeguards Rule (16 CFR Part 314) — encryption, access controls/MFA, employee training, vendor oversight, and a written incident-response plan. A designated Qualified Individual — Manager (e.g., the General Manager/Owner) — oversees our information-security program and reviews it with ownership at least annually.

8. How we share information

We share information with service providers who help us operate (telephony/SMS platform, CRM, scheduling, financing sources), bound to protect it and use it only for that purpose; and as required by law or to protect our rights. We do not sell your personal information, and (per §5) we do not share mobile opt-in/consent data with third parties.

Categories of service providers we use include: cloud AI language-model providers (to power Gambo's conversational responses); voice-AI and call-recording providers (to transcribe and record calls per §6); SMS/voice telephony providers (to send and receive texts and calls); and payment processors (such as Stripe, for any online payments or deposits you choose to make). Each is bound to use your information only to provide that service to us.

9. Data security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information, and for financial information we maintain the FTC Safeguards Rule program described in §7. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

10. Data breach notification

If we discover a breach of personal information (including encrypted information whose encryption key was also acquired), we will notify affected individuals as required by Washington law (RCW 19.255) — in the most expedient time possible and no later than 30 days after discovery — and, for breaches involving the NPI of 500 or more consumers, notify the FTC within 30 days as required by the GLBA Safeguards Rule. If a breach affects more than 500 Washington residents, we will also notify the Washington State Attorney General within 30 days, as required by RCW 19.255.010.

11. Your choices and rights

  • Texts: reply STOP (or OPT OUT/UNSUBSCRIBE/CANCEL/END/QUIT/REVOKE) to stop, HELP for help, or contact us to opt out by any reasonable means (honored within 10 business days).
  • Do-Not-Contact list: we maintain an internal Do-Not-Contact suppression list. Any request to stop marketing contact — reply STOP, a call, or an email — is added immediately and honored permanently. Before initiating any future outbound marketing campaign, we will scrub our call/text lists against the National Do Not Call Registry. To be added, contact andrewg@gamblinmotors.com or (360) 825-2200.
  • Marketing email: use the unsubscribe link or contact us.
  • Financial-information sharing: we share your NPI only within federal exceptions (see §7), so there is no GLBA opt-out to exercise under our current sharing practices.
  • Washington residents: you have the right to receive notice of a breach of your personal information under RCW 19.255. Contact andrewg@gamblinmotors.com with any privacy question or request.

12. Data retention

We retain records only as long as needed for the purpose collected: call recordings for 90 days (§6); financing/NPI records as required by law (e.g., ~7 years under Regulation B); CRM/contact records until you opt out or for as long as you remain a customer or as required by law; and mobile numbers only while you remain subscribed or as required by law.

13. Children

Our services are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect their information.
