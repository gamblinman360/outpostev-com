Privacy Policy

Effective June 9, 2026

This policy covers outpostev.com and the "Text Outpost EV" texting program at (360) 284-1554, operated by Gamblin LLC, doing business as Art Gamblin Motors and Outpost EV, 205 Roosevelt Ave, Enumclaw, WA 98022.

1. What we collect

  • When you text us: your phone number, the contents of your messages, and any information you choose to share (such as your name or the vehicle you're interested in).
  • When you use a contact form: the information you submit, such as your name, phone number, email, and message.

2. How we use it

We use this information for one purpose: serving you as a customer — answering your questions, responding to your requests, scheduling test drives you ask for, and having our team follow up. We do not use texting data for advertising profiles, and we do not send marketing blasts through this program.

3. SMS privacy — the short version

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties, excluding the service providers who operate our texting platform on our behalf.

The service providers who deliver our messages (our messaging platform and telecommunications carriers) process message data solely to provide that service. We never sell your information, to anyone.

4. Retention

We keep conversation records only as long as needed to serve you and to meet our legal and bookkeeping obligations, then delete them in the normal course of business.

5. Your choices

  • Opt out of texts at any time by replying STOP.
  • Ask us what we have on file, or ask us to delete it, by calling (360) 825-2200 or visiting the store.

6. Contact

Questions about this policy: call (360) 825-2200 or write to Gamblin LLC, 205 Roosevelt Ave, Enumclaw, WA 98022.
